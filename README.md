## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-03-07**

### Data We Access

SPARTA requests access to your Google Calendar in order to:
- Write pomodoro session records as calendar events
- Read your upcoming events to display countdowns

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** send any data to external servers
- We do **not** store your calendar data outside your browser
- We do **not** share your data with any third parties
- All data is stored locally in your browser's extension storage only

### Google Calendar Access

- **Write**: Records pomodoro sessions as calendar events
- **Read**: Retrieves upcoming events to show time-until-next-event in the toolbar
- You can revoke access at any time from the extension settings or from your [Google Account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
